Team Development: BEBEJI PLAZA Web Application

As we at students of computer science, we have designed this application to support young entrepreneurs in the field of buying and selling phones in Bebeji plaza Gusau. We have conducted extensive research on how we can improve the telephone business, and how we can help develop small businesses in our state of Zamfara.

Our mission:

**Business Improvement:** We aim to help buyers and sellers facilitate their transactions through the use of technology. **Supporting Small Businesses:** Our application will help small businesses grow by providing them with a wide market online. **Easier Transactions:** We designed the application to streamline transactions, reduce disputes, and ensure business integrity.

We expect that this technology will bring a positive change to youth businesses in Zamfara state, helping to develop the economy and improve the lives of the people.
